Medieval Modpack installatoin steps:

  1. - Install the CurseForge App and the Medieval Modpack.
  2. - Make sure to allocate 8 - 10 gigabytes of RAM.
  3. - Delete and replace your mods folder with the updated mod list.

(Optional)

  1. - You can download my preset binds, shaders, and settings for an even easier experience.
  2. Distant Horizons is a mod that allows you to substantially increase your render distance and is highly recommended to use with shaders.
  3. - Download and place them within your mods folder.
  4. - You will need these specific shaders in order for them to work with Distant Horizons.
  5. - Additionally, you can download a large pregenerated area so the experience is seamless.
